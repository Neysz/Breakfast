Best places to have breakfast

(according to me)

El Trapiche

The best chicharrones in salsa verde in town. Great café de olla.

Panamá

Ridiculously big portions. Everything tastes kinda the same but still pretty good.

El Huanacaxtle

Second best chicharrones in salsa verde. (Man, I really love chicharrones en salsa verde).

El mesón de los Laureanos

Food just keep coming. I’m so full, oh my God those were just the courtesies.

Casa Benell

Get the red Chilaquiles with chicharrón de la Ramos (I told u I loved them) you’re welcome.

The Tipper

This one is more like a nostalgia thing, but it’s pretty good though.
